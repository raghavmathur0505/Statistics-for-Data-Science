Dataset description:

This dataset contains heights in inches of the singers in the New York Choral Society in 1979. The data are grouped according to voice part. There are four voice parts, namely, Bass, Tenor, Alto, and Soprano. The vocal range for each voice part increases in pitch from Bass to Soprano.

About the Project:
	1) Performed an exploratory analysis of the data by examining the distributions of the heights of the singers in the four groups
	2) Formulated this problem as a test of appropriate hypothesis.
	3) Test Statistic, p-value, Monte Carlo simulation, Confidence Level

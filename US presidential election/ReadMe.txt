# Map of State-by-State Percentage Vote Share of Presidential Candidates in the 2016 US Presidential Election. 

Project description:

	• Technology: R
	• Designed a map of state-by-state percentage vote share of President Trump and Hillary Clinton in
	the 2016 US presidential election. The map represents the margin of percentage of votes by which President elect Donald Trump won over Hillary Clinton in US Election-2016. 
	• Analyzed the dataset and mapped the votes data into different states and represented it by shades of Red and Blue colors. 
	The data is stored in a CSV file.

It uses shades of red for Donald Trump and shades of blue for Hillary Clinton.
